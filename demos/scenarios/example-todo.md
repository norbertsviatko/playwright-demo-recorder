# Todo App Demo

A short demo showing basic todo list interactions: adding items, completing them, and deleting.

## Flow

> TITLE: Todo App | Adding, completing, and deleting tasks

1. Open the app
2. Pause briefly on the empty state

> CAPTION: Let's add our first task

3. Click on the input field
4. Type "Buy groceries"
5. Click the "Add" button

> CAPTION: Adding a second task

6. Click on the input field
7. Type "Walk the dog"
8. Click "Add"

> CAPTION: And a third one

9. Click on the input field
10. Type "Read a book"
11. Click "Add"

> PAUSE: 1000

> CAPTION_HOLD: Three tasks added — let's complete one

12. Check the checkbox on "Buy groceries"
13. Pause to show the strikethrough effect

> CAPTION_CLEAR

> HIGHLIGHT: [data-testid="counter"] | 2 remaining

14. Pause on the counter

> CAPTION: Now let's remove the completed task

15. Click "Delete" on "Buy groceries"

> PAUSE: 1000

> CAPTION: Clean and simple!

16. Final pause on the remaining tasks
